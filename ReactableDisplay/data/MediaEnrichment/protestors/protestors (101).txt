Turbulence in Turkey: Massive protest sweeps Istanbul's Taksim Sq for 2nd day. Despite the authorities decision to allow thousands to flood onto the square, the main subway gateway to Taksim, the central station in the city's metro network, has reportedly been shut down in an effort to keep more people from reaching the ongoing protests.
