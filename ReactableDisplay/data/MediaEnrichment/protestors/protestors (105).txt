Taksim Gezi Parkı Direnişi 4. Gün / Resistance Against Destruction Of Taksim Gezi Park Day 4. 31. 05. 2013 Early in the morning police evicted the protesters from Gezi Park and surrounded the park with the barriers. The Protesters came back at 10:00 am. The protesters then continued to Taksim Square where many people joined them. They were sitting peacefuly when the police attacted them with tear gas. That is how it all started.
